Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction epic that follows a team of astronauts as they venture through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. As Earth faces environmental collapse, Cooper, a former NASA pilot, must make impossible choices for the future of his family and the survival of the human race.
