Summary

"Interstellar" is a science fiction epic directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Cooper, a former NASA pilot, who embarks on a space mission through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet as Earth becomes uninhabitable. It's a powerful tale of survival, time, love, and the unknown.

Actors

Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Dr. Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, Michael Caine as Professor Brand, and Mackenzie Foy as young Murph.
