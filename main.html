Summary

*Interstellar* explores the story of a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. Led by Cooper, a former NASA pilot, the mission becomes both a journey through space and a reflection on time, gravity, and the bonds of love.
