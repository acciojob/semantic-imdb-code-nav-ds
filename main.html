Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious through dreams. He is given a chance at redemption by performing an impossible task — planting an idea into someone’s mind, a process known as "inception."

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, Ken Watanabe as Saito, and Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer.
