Summary

"The Dark Knight" is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. In this critically acclaimed sequel to "Batman Begins," Batman faces his most cunning opponent yet — the Joker, a criminal mastermind who seeks to plunge Gotham City into chaos. As Batman, Commissioner Gordon, and Harvey Dent attempt to dismantle the criminal underworld, they are pushed to their ethical and emotional limits.

Actors

Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman, Heath Ledger as The Joker, Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face, Gary Oldman as Commissioner Gordon, Michael Caine as Alfred, and Morgan Freeman as Lucius Fox.
