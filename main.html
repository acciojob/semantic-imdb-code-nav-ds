Summary

Fearing the actions of a god-like superhero left unchecked, Gotham City’s own formidable, forceful vigilante, Batman, takes on Metropolis’s most revered savior, Superman, while the world wrestles with what kind of hero it really needs. As the two icons clash, a new threat, Doomsday, emerges — putting mankind in greater danger than ever before.
