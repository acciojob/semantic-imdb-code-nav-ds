Summary

*Avengers: Endgame* is the culmination of over a decade of Marvel storytelling. After the devastating events of *Infinity War*, the remaining Avengers work to undo the damage caused by Thanos. Through time travel, sacrifice, and teamwork, they attempt to bring back the lost half of the universe and face Thanos one final time.
