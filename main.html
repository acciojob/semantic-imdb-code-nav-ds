Summary

"Inception" is a sci-fi thriller where Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is given a chance to have his criminal history erased. His mission is not to steal an idea, but to plant one in someone’s mind — a process called inception. As the layers of dreams unfold, the boundary between reality and imagination begins to blur.
